Heading number 2

There are about 200 words in a paragraph, but this can vary widely. Most paragraphs focus on a single idea that's expressed with and introductory sentence, then followed by two or more supporting sentences about the idea. A short paragraph may not reach even 50 words while long paragraphs can be over 400 words long, but generally speaking they tend to be approximately 200 words in length.
